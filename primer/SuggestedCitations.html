Suggested Citations

Suggested citation for version 1.1 of the SDD standard (= recommended for implementations):

  • Hagedorn, G.; Thiele, K.; Morris, R. & Heidorn, P. B. 2006. The Structured Descriptive Data (SDD) w3c-xml-schema, version 1.1. http://rs.tdwg.org/UBIF/2006/rddl.html.

Suggested citation for version 1.0 of the SDD standard (= voted TDWG standard):

  • Hagedorn, G.; Thiele, K.; Morris, R. & Heidorn, P. B. 2005. The Structured Descriptive Data (SDD) w3c-xml-schema, version 1.0. http://rs.tdwg.org/UBIF/2005/rddl.html.

Suggested citation for the SDD primer:

  • Thiele, Kevin & Sharp, Donovan 2006. SDD part 0: Introduction and primer to the SDD standard. https://sdd.tdwg.org/.
